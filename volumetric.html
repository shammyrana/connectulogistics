📐 How is Volumetric Weight Calculated?

Volumetric weight (also called dimensional weight) is calculated using this formula:

Volumetric Weight (kg) = (Length × Width × Height in cm) ÷ 5000

Couriers use the higher of the actual weight and the volumetric weight to determine the chargeable weight of your shipment. This is standard practice in the international courier industry.
